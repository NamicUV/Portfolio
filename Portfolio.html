Better Cheddar

This project consists of a simple budget management web site that will help users develop better spending habits in the hope of lowering the percent of people living paycheck to paycheck. Users are able to log their spending and also have the ability to edit and delete any logs as they see fit. Once they insert their monthly income, calculations will be automatically made and shown, allowing users to see how they should divide their income based on their selected plan. Users progress will be presented as a bar graph which shows the user where they stand based on thier selected plan.

Skills Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Bootstrap, Jquery(AJAX)
